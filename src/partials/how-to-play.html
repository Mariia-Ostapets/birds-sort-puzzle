How to Play

  • 1Sort the Birds

    Move birds into their corresponding spaces according to their color, type, or pattern.

  • 2Complete the Puzzle

    Clear the board by successfully sorting all the birds.

  • 3Earn Stars

    Collect stars as rewards for completing puzzles, unlocking new levels.

  • 4Challenge Yourself

    As the levels increase, the puzzles become more complex and require better planning.
